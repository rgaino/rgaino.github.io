Patroca

Patroca is a social bartering service that helps you trade things with people from your social network. I developed this app for iPhone as a hobby project. It uses Parse as a backed-as-a-service tool. It makes use of their database, cloud code and push notification services.

I didn't write a single line of code for the backend, this app has no custom backend at all except for Parse.

Get it here.


  • Client: Self-made
  • Date: July 2014

Bis Prank Machine

For the most loved chocolate in Brazil, we completely hacked a vending machine to be remotely controlled. We then put into a college lobby and filmed the people interacting with it.

The machine was controlled by a famous comedian from a safe distance. He could see and hear everything, and speak with a modulated voice through the vending machine's speakers. He could also drop chocolate and some items to play with the people, like balloons, masks and a lime (yeah, a lime, watch the video below).


  • Client: Mondelez
  • Date: August 2014

Nike - Ouse Ser Brasileiro

“Dare to be Brazilian” showcases how every Brazilian has a unique style and way of seeing the game. But when they come together as one, they are an unstoppable force.

Wieden+Kennedy São Paulo created Nike's biggest campaign ever in Brazil showing the most famous player's silhouettes.

The campaign extends to out of home and digital showing each players individual mindset of how they play the game with a silhouette of their head.

Consumers are invited to be a part of the campaign and create their own silhouette for social media or on customized t-shirts.

My role was of interactive producer, working closely with the creative team to ensure their vision, with account to be on time, budget and to Nike and W+K standards, and the production company responsible for the development.

The website runs perfectly in mobile devices with only HTML/CSS and Javascript, only using Flash on the desktop to capture the user picture for compatibility reasons.

The website is no longer active, but you can see the screenshots and film below.


  • Client: Nike
  • Date: December 2013

TIM - CHamada Imperdível

TIM launches an unprecedented action to publicize the Chamada Imperdível (Unmissed Call) promotion, which gives away, among other prizes, R$3,000 per month for life. The daring campaign stands out for its surreal and fun construction. The digital campaign has over 20 interactive videos that emphasize the good humor of the Infinity plan ads and brings a new tone to promotional advertising.

The videos were shown randomly and can only be watched through user interactions and shared through the hotsite. The website was also developed to be embedabble in blog posts, which made it extremely shareable.

The website is no longer active but you can watch the videos here.

AT&T JustUs

AT&T's JustUs is a series of artfully designed Live Wallpapers that work by surfacing messages from your favorite contacts in a playful and beautiful manner. DOJO was responsible for the design and behavior of these wallpapers that are now available for free for any Android device on the AT&T store.

My role on this project was project manager/producer, working since the conception, coordinating with the artists involved in creating each piece, to managing the development of the animated Flash prototypes that served as a reference for the Android developers.

Lucasfilm's Red Tails

DOJO was hired to build the website and digital campaign for Lucasfilm's Red Tails, an epic movie about the Tuskegee Airmen. On the website, users are given the chance to fly a P-51 Mustang and fight the Nazi germans. Those who are afraid of heights can go to office base and explore the history of the Tuskegee Airmen and learn more about the characters and the movie.


My role on the campaign was of project manager/producer. I coordinated the creative team's efforts and the production of the website and digital campaign.


The website is no longer available.

  • Date: January 2012

LG dLite

For the launch of the new LG dLite, DOJO created a campaign made of a music video, a website, and digital banners. On the website, users are able to build their own music video to the campaign's soundtrack, and share their story with their friends and family. Also, an augmented reality game lets you use your hands to play "light pads", a virtual instrument that you can use to play along the campaign's song.


The campaign went on to receive one gold and four silvers on the 2011 SF Bay Area Addy Awards.


My role on the campaign was of project manager/producer. I coordinated the creative team's efforts and the production of the website and digital banners.









  • Client: LG
  • Date: August 2009
