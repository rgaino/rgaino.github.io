Smart Beats

Smart Beats is a voice-activated smart speaker, your very own bottle-shaped personalized party planner. It suggests the best bars and restaurants, and even calls you an Uber if you're planning on drinking. It gives you tips on how to dress based on the weather, it plays awesome music and even orders food and drinks for your party.

We produced 25 units in partnership with MediaMonks. 20 units were given away on sweepstakes.

This was the most challenging and gratifying project I've worked on at Wieden+Kennedy. Every detail, paint, shape, color, speaker quality, LED lights, tone of voice of the device, everything was carefully executed. The software also was no small feat. We used APIs from IBM Watson, Twilio, Spotify, Uber, Weather and Zé Delivery (Ambev's own delivery service). The device speaks Brazilian Portuguese exclusively, which was also a big challenge given the limited functionality of speech recognition services, and many common slangs and anglicisms.

Below you can watch the campaign video, and a demo that shows that this thing is for real.



  • Client: Ambev, Skol Beats
  • Date: May 2018

Nike #falapoucojogamuito

When Brazil's biggest soccer team was technically champion by points before even the end of the championship, we launched #Falapoucojogamuito for Nike, wich translates to something like # TalkalittlePlayalot.

Corinthians had a rough start in the nationals and everyone made fun of them for that, but they got their shit together, and 2 games away from the final they were already champions by points. So when they won the game that sealed the championship, we opened a page where every fan could allow our app to publish a single post in their timeline during the next game telling their rivals to shut up. The post had the image of the players shushing everyone, and it said:

our silence never made so much noise
silence please, our 6th title has arrived

During only 2 days between games we collected 6879 authorization tokens and when the next game started we posted on every timeline on an avalanche of celebration.
